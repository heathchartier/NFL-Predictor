📋 Situational Rules Reference
These are your collected betting tendencies. High-value rules are flagged automatically on game cards as alerts. Verify these periodically — some may have evolved since collected.

Step 1 — Fetch

  • Select season and week, click Fetch Games + Stats
  • Auto-filled: matchups, spread, records, PF, PA, turnovers, all 4 ranking stats, yards/play, 3rd down %, time of possession

Step 2 — Enter Spread / Total

  • Enter the spread from your preferred book — you can enter spread only, total only, or both
  • Calculation works with either or both — live recalculates as you type
  • You can also adjust any auto-fetched stat manually

Step 3 — Calculate & Rank

  • Click Calculate & Rank — games rank 16 (most confident) down to 1 (least confident)
  • Your formula: PF/PA base scores → ranking adjustments (1-9 diff = 1pt, 10-19 = 2pt, 20-31 = 3pt) → result
  • Ranking logic: Largest gap between your estimated spread and Vegas line = rank 16 (most confident). Example: your model says 8, Vegas says 2 → diff of 6 → high rank. Small diff = low rank.
  • Underdog picks are flagged in purple
  • Situational alerts appear automatically based on game context

Step 4 — Enter Results

  • After games, go to Enter Results, add final scores, save
  • Win% tracks overall and by confidence tier (1–5, 6–10, 11–16)

Early Season Stat Blending

  • Week 1: 70% prior season final stats + 30% preseason stats — no current season data exists yet
  • Week 2: 60% prior + 20% preseason + 20% current (1 game of real data)
  • Week 3: 40% prior + 10% preseason + 50% current (2 games of real data)
  • Week 4+: 100% current season stats only
  • All blending happens automatically in the background. A ⚗ badge appears on blended games.
  • Hover the ⚗ icon on any team to see the exact blend breakdown applied.
  • These weights are our starting point — deep analysis will tell us if different ratios work better.
  • If home PF avg > vis PA avg: home_base = (h_pf - h_pf/v_pa) / 2
  • If home PF avg < vis PA avg: home_base = h_pf + v_pa/h_pf
  • If home PA avg > vis PF avg: vis_base = v_pf + h_pa/v_pf
  • If home PA avg < vis PF avg: vis_base = (v_pf - v_pf/h_pa) / 2
  • Each team's pass/rush rank vs opponent's pass/rush def rank → ±1/2/3 pts
  • Spread diff = |estimated spread − Vegas line| → larger diff = higher confidence rank (your model disagrees more with the market)
